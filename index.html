a cute fairy tale about kittens

Once upon a time there were three little fluffy kittens named Tom, Baby and Mittens. They had very beautiful fluffy fur and most of all they loved to play at the doorstep of the house – to jump and swim in the dust. One day their mother, Mrs. Tabitha Twitchit, was expecting guests for a cup of tea. And before their arrival, she had to bathe and dress up her minx.First she washed their faces with a sponge.Then she combed their fur coats… And finally fluffed their tails and whiskers. Tom was the most playful of all the kittens, and he didn't really like all this preening and kept pulling away from mom and scratching. Mrs. Tabitha dressed Baby and Mitt in fresh dresses and aprons, and then took out a whole pile of clothes for Tom from the closet. There were a lot of different things there, but Tom didn't like them at all – it was so uncomfortable in them! Mom was surprised, it turns out Tom managed to grow up and get better during this time, that his shirt and pants barely converged on him, and several buttons even broke off and she had to sew them on again.

The three musketeers

I have three cats living at home, whose names are Athos, Porthos and Aramis. I decided to call them that because I really love this book. The first cat is Athos, he loves to drink valerian and will find it everywhere, wherever it is. Porthos the cat loves to eat and the clink of coins. And the cat Aramis is constantly trying to find a kitty, tells "poems", and if you refuse him something, he will stand in the corner and eat nothing.

Cutlet

I stopped by my mother's, I look — she's laughing, tears are already flowing. Told: fried cutlets, putting them ready in a plate. Our cat was sitting next to us and pretended that he was not eating cutlets, even looked in the other direction. The mother was going to put the finished ones in a plate, looks — one is missing. The cat is sitting in the same place, looking at her. They played peek-a-boo for a while, then the cat screeched away, leaving the steaming cutlet in the place where he was sitting. Stole it and couldn't think of anything better than to sit on it.
